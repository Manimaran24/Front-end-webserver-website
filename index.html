FAQ

+

How to upadate website?

Edit your website files or use a content management system (CMS) to make changes. Test the updates thoroughly, then deploy them to the live server.

+

is domain is provided free?

Domains are generally not free; most require a purchase or annual renewal. Some website builders offer free domains with their plans.

+

How much will it cost?

Domain registration typically costs between $10 to $20 per year. Premium domains or specialty TLDs can be more expensive.

+

How long it takes to design?

Designing a website can take from a few days to several months, depending on complexity and customization. Simple sites may be faster, while intricate ones require more time.

+

is it too friendly?

It depends on your target audience. A friendly design fosters engagement and trust, but balance is key to ensure professionalism.
